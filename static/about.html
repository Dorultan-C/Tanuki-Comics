About Us

Dive into the enchanting universe of Tanuki Comics, where every anime enthusiast's dreams come true. 🚀 Our store, nestled in the lively and picturesque city of Sitges, just a short journey from the renowned Barcelona, is an anime wonderland that captures the heart and soul of Japanese animation and manga culture. At Tanuki Comics, we're passionate about providing our valued customers with an ever-expanding collection of top-quality products. From limited edition figurines and expertly crafted collectibles to an extensive array of comic books and graphic novels, our selection caters to fans of all ages and tastes. 📘🎁 But that's not all! Our shelves are also filled with an exciting assortment of accessories, clothing, and merchandise that let you express your love for your favorite characters and series in style. And if you're searching for the perfect gift for an anime aficionado in your life, our knowledgeable and friendly staff are always on hand to help you find the ideal present that will leave them speechless. 🛍️😍 So why wait? Embark on an unforgettable adventure and immerse yourself in the captivating world of anime at Tanuki Comics today. We can't wait to welcome you into our community of passionate fans and share our love for this extraordinary art form! 🌟🎊
